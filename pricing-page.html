Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?
Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. No lock-in contracts. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.
What if I only need 2–3 tools?
Subscribe to individual tools à la carte. Most customers start with 2–3 tools and add more as they see the value.
Do you offer a free trial?
Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to get started.
Are there setup fees or hidden costs?
No setup fees, no hidden costs. The price you see is what you pay. All features included in each tool, no surprises.
What support do I get?
All plans include email support. Growth and Complete plans get priority support and strategy calls. Tools are designed to be self-explanatory — no training needed.
Do tools work on mobile?
Yes — TradeAI Pro is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Install it to your phone's home screen for a native app experience. Works on iOS and Android.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards and debit cards via Stripe. Invoicing is available for Complete plan customers.
